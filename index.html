FAQ

Common Questions

Is my money secure on Aurelia Trade?
+
Absolutely. We employ bank-grade 256-bit AES encryption, store 95% of client assets in geographically distributed cold wallets, and carry $250M in insurance coverage. Two-factor authentication is mandatory, and our security operations center monitors the platform around the clock for anomalous activity. We are regulated in 28 jurisdictions.
What assets can I trade?
+
Aurelia Trade supports over 500 trading pairs across six asset classes: cryptocurrencies, US & international equities, forex (80+ pairs), commodities (gold, silver, oil, gas), index CFDs (S&P 500, NASDAQ, Dow, FTSE, Nikkei), and ETFs. New assets are added regularly based on trader demand.
How fast are withdrawals processed?
+
Crypto withdrawals are processed within 1 hour. Fiat withdrawals (USD, EUR, GBP) via wire transfer settle in 1–2 business days. Pro and Institutional clients enjoy same-day fiat withdrawals. There are no withdrawal fees for amounts above $1,000.
Do you support copy trading?
+
Yes — copy trading is available on Pro and Institutional plans. You can browse our verified leaderboard of top performers, filter by asset class, risk level, and track record, and mirror their trades automatically with customizable position sizing and drawdown limits.
Is there a mobile app?
+
Yes. Aurelia Trade is available on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play) with full feature parity to the web platform — including live charts, order management, copy trading, push notifications for price alerts, and biometric authentication. Your data syncs instantly across all devices.
